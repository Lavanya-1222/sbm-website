How to Save Your Gadget?

📱

Invest in a Quality Phone Case

A good case absorbs impact shock and prevents screen cracks from accidental drops.

🔒

Download Only from Trusted Sources

Avoid pirated software and unofficial app stores — they're the #1 source of malware infections.

🛡️

Set Up Strong Biometric Lock

Use fingerprint or face recognition to protect your data if your device is lost or stolen.

📡

Disable Bluetooth & Wi-Fi When Idle

Leaving these on drains battery and creates security vulnerabilities for hackers.

💨

Clean Your Laptop Vents Regularly

Dust buildup is the #1 cause of overheating and premature component failure in laptops.
