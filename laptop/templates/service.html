FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take? +
Most repairs are completed same-day or within 24 hours. Complex motherboard repairs or data recovery may take 2-3 business days. We always provide an accurate timeline after free diagnosis.
Do you use genuine spare parts? +
Yes, we use only OEM and manufacturer-approved spare parts for all repairs. We never use cheap aftermarket components that could cause further damage to your device.
Is there a warranty on repairs? +
Absolutely! All our repairs come with a minimum 90-day warranty. If the same issue reoccurs within the warranty period, we fix it free of charge, no questions asked.
Can you recover data from a physically damaged drive? +
Yes! We have specialized tools and cleanroom techniques for physically damaged drives. Our data recovery success rate is over 95% even for severely damaged media.
Do you offer pick-up and delivery service? +
Yes, we offer free doorstep pickup and delivery within Solapur city for our Professional and Business plan customers. We also accept courier shipments from other cities.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking. For business clients, we also offer invoice-based monthly billing with credit terms.
